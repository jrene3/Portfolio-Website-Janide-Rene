BeFriendly

Role: Sole UI/UX and Visual Designer

Problem

Friends keep us company through the ups and downs of our lives and help us grow. As a child, making friends is practically second nature. But as we grow older, our fear and social anxiety can keep us from making new friends. How can we help people who have social anxiety step out and make new friends?

Solution

BeFriendly is the new social app designed to help adults make new friends from the comfort of their smartphones. With BeFriendly, users are able to match with others with shared interests, send messages, and connect with their communities through local events. BeFriendly is the solution for adults to step out of their social anxiety by creating opportunities to create local friendships.

Competitive Analysis

I did a competitive analysis in order to understand the current market of apps available for adults to connect and make new friends. This helped to guide me on features to include in BeFriendly.

Hey! Vina

Hey! Vina is a matchmaking app tailored to women wanting to meet new friends and join communities of people like them. With Hey! Vina, users swipe to make new friends, take quizzes to find better matches, and read lifestyle articles.

Bumble BFF

Bumble BFF, a branch of Bumble dating, allows its users to customize their profiles and share their interests, connect their social media, and match with similar users in their area to make new friends.

Meetup

People have turned to Meetup to meet people, make friends, find support, grow a business, and explore their interests through thousands of events happening every day.

User Flow

Once I decided on the tasks that users would be able do, I created the flow users would take to complete tasks.

  • Create a profile
  • Browse and match with others
  • Send a direct message
  • RSVP for events

Wireframes

After honing in on my users and their goals I created wireframes to visualize my initial layouts which translated into the first iteration of mid-fidelity wireframes.

Iteration

During user testing, I collected feedback to improve the design and functionality of the app.
